Ceremony & Party

The Altar Electric

Our ceremony will be held at The Altar Electric. Tucked in Abbotsford’s creative heart, this intimate space is full of personality, good vibes, and just the right amount of cheek. Genuinely, we both walked in and fell in love within seconds. We can’t wait to say “I do” here.

Stingrays at Bodriggy

After the ceremony, we’ll be heading to Stingrays — the cocktail saloon nestled inside Bodriggy Brewery. It’s a space with incredible drinks, delicious food, and plenty of room for a bootscoot. We’d love for you to join us there to eat, drink, and dance into the night.
